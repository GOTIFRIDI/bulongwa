Admission To The Institute

The Institute is currently offering Diploma Course in Clinical Dentistry and Certificate in Nursing and midwifery.

  1. Application

    2. Application for the year 2017/2018 academic year is through the Institute. An application fee of 30,000/= tsh is paid through bank-BULONGWA HEALTH SCIENCES INSTITUTE ,ACC.NO.0150226058000 CRADB OR 60403500081 NMB. Then application form is filled and submitted to the Institute.

  2. Entry Requirements

    3. The entry requirements are adopted from MoHSW and NACTE.

    1. Dental Therapy

      A pass in ordinary level secondary school for the sciences subjects of Biology C, Chemistry C and Physics D will be eligible. Pass in Mathematics and English is added advantage.

    2. Nursing And Midwfery And Clinical Assistants

      A pass in Biology D, Chemistry D, and Physics D.A pass in Mathematics and English is an added advantage.

    3. Community Health

      A pass in four subjects including BIOLOGY, EXCEPT RELIGIOUS SUBJECTS.
